About Us

Built for Students, By Students

College Event Hub was born from a simple frustration — students missing out on amazing events because there was no central place to discover them.

We built a platform that aggregates events from across campuses, makes registration seamless, and helps students build a richer, more connected college life. From hackathons to cultural fests, workshops to sports tournaments — we have it all.
